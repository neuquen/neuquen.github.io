ECONOMY-WIDE IMPACT

Robotics has the potential to impact virtually every sector in the US economy, from "care-bots" in health care, to driverless trucks in transportation, and "robo-analysts" in finance.

In 2015, the Bank of America Merril Lynch analyzed investment opportunities related to the "robot revolution" across major sectors in the US economy. They estimated that robotics and artificial intelligence would become a $153 billion industry by 2020.
